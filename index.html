I'm a Front-End Web Developer with 4 years of experience building high-quality, responsive websites for a wide range of clients. My commercial experience is centred around developing custom WordPress themes using HTML, CSS, Sass, JavaScript, PHP, and jQuery, while I'm actively expanding my skills in React through personal projects and continuous learning.

I enjoy bringing designs to life with clean, maintainable code and a strong attention to detail, ensuring every website is pixel-perfect, accessible, and performs seamlessly across devices. Having delivered projects for local councils and public sector organisations, I have a solid understanding of accessibility standards and creating intuitive user experiences that are clear, inclusive, and easy to navigate. With experience working from professional design files and a good understanding of UI and UX principles, I'm passionate about building modern interfaces that look great and provide an excellent user experience.
